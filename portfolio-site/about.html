My Profile

Designer, builder, and maker of things; builder of highly functional teams that work best by working together. Instructor, Interim Director of Undergraduate Program, Co-Chair Board of Advisors, ATLAS Institute; Eagle Scout, Scoutmaster; VP of Product Management, and member of the SketchUp startup team.

I make products that people love because they "...just work." I make teams that people love to work on because they "just work," too.

Here is my Resume. If you need some design support, some product management, or you just need help getting your team working together a little better than before, I'm keen to help.

You can Contact Me through this site, especially if you're looking for a phone number, email address, or physical mailing address. I'm always happy to chat, though I've had to become more selective in how much I expose to the wilds of the internet. You understand I'm sure.

In these times of cultural change, knowing where I stand on questions of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion is sometimes helpful. I've distilled that thinking into a concise Diversity Statement, which I invite you to review.

Lately, I've taken a more serious look at my Teaching Principles, and I invite you to have a look at them as well. While I have considerable executive-level experience with software development, I have a strong interest in teaching as well. I believe that to really know a topic, one should be able to teach it competently.

And lastly, I've given some particular thought to the principles under which I exercise my leadership. If you're interested, I have a Leadership Manifesto.
