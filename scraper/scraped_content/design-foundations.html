Deep Design in the age of Generative AI

In 2020, I wrote a book outlining some ideas about maintaining a sketching practice in our digital age. While I was happy with the outcome, I felt like I could have gone further. Last year, I began gathering material for a new book, a general primer on "deep design" in the age of artificial intelligence. I think there might be a new book in this, though I'm not in a hurry to finish it.
For now, you're welcome to wander in the Deep Design garden.
